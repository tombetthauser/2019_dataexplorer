Welcome to the SotA 2019 Data Explorer 2.0.1

An interface for cross-referencing raw data from the 2019 State of the Arts Survey results.

Below are two drop down selectors that choose any two questions from the survey to cross-reference results from. "Render Data Table" will display a table of numbers indicating how many survey participants chose the respective responses that line up vertically and horizontally from the number's location.

The survey covered 89 questions, meaning that there are 7,832 possible tables to explore (89!/(89-2)!). If you would like to explore the data visualizations (graphs, pie charts etc) for individual question response sets check out the 2019 survey website linked below.

full survey website --> http://www.sotasurvey.org/2019
